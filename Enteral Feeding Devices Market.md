<h3>Market Overview</h3>
<p>The <strong>enteral feeding devices market</strong> is witnessing steady growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic illnesses, rising demand for home-based care, and technological advancements in feeding devices. Enteral feeding is a preferred method for delivering essential nutrients to patients unable to consume food orally due to medical conditions like cancer, gastrointestinal disorders, and neurological impairments.</p>

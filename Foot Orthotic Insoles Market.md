<h3><strong>Market Drivers</strong></h3>
<ol>
<li><strong>Increasing Prevalence of Foot Disorders:</strong> Conditions such as plantar fasciitis, bunions, and flat feet are on the rise, fueling the demand for orthotic insoles to manage pain and improve foot function.</li>
<li><strong>Growing Sports and Fitness Activities:</strong> The increasing participation in sports and fitness activities worldwide has heightened the demand for performance-enhancing and injury-preventing orthotic insoles.</li>
<li><strong>Technological Advancements in Material Design:</strong> Innovations such as 3D printing and advanced materials like composite carbon fibers and gel-based insoles are driving market growth.</li>
<li><strong>Aging Population:</strong> The elderly population is highly prone to foot-related ailments, further boosting the demand for orthotic solutions.</li>
</ol>

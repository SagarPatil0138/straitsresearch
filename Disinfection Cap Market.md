<h3>Market Definition and Trends</h3>
<p>The Disinfection Cap Market comprises medical devices designed to prevent infections associated with catheter use by sealing and disinfecting access points. These caps are primarily used in healthcare settings such as hospitals and clinics to reduce the risk of bloodstream infections.</p>
<p>Recent market trends indicate a growing emphasis on infection control protocols, with hospitals and clinics increasingly adopting disinfection caps to meet stringent regulatory standards. Additionally, advancements in antimicrobial technology and the integration of user-friendly designs are enhancing product efficacy and adoption rates. The surge in hospital-acquired infections (HAIs) has further fueled the demand for reliable disinfection solutions.</p>

<h3><strong>Market Definition</strong></h3>
<p>Cell therapy involves the administration of live cellular material to restore, repair, or replace damaged tissue or cells within the body. This cutting-edge treatment is widely used in oncology, regenerative medicine, and immunotherapy, offering promising results in treating diseases such as cancer, autoimmune disorders, and neurological conditions. The market encompasses various products, including cell engineering tools, culture media, reagents, and cell processing equipment, which facilitate the development and commercialization of cell-based treatments.</p>

</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Growing Demand for Stem Cell Therapy:</strong> The regenerative medicine sector is witnessing increased adoption of stem cell-based therapies for conditions such as spinal cord injuries, cardiovascular diseases, and degenerative disorders.</p>
